Attack on Titan
**Attack on Titan** (_Shingeki no Kyojin_) is a _Japanese anime series_ about humanity’s fight for survival against giant creatures called **Titans**.

## Main Characters
1. **Eren Yeager** – The main hero who wants to destroy all Titans.  
2. **Mikasa Ackerman** – A brave and loyal warrior who protects Eren.  
3. **Armin Arlert** – A smart and strategic thinker who values peace.
